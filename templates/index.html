About the Project

This Quick Draw game is powered by a machine learning model trained on Google's Quick Draw dataset, which contains millions of drawings across hundreds of categories.

Our model has been trained on 50 randomly selected categories from this dataset, achieving an impressive 87.7% accuracy in recognizing doodles.

The challenge is simple: we'll give you a category, and you have 40 seconds to draw it. Then our AI will try to recognize what you've drawn!
